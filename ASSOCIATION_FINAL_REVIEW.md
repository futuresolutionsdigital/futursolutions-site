- [ ] Hero headline readable with natural line breaks (not narrow "totem pole" wrapping)

## 8. Pitch Notes

### What the Demo Proves

**Membership Conversion**
A tiered membership system with audience segmentation, benefit comparison, social proof, and clear conversion pathways that make the value of joining obvious.

**Advocacy Command Center**
Policy priorities, action alerts, legislative tracking, Lobby Day promotion, and deep-dive issue pages that show how an association can mobilize members on professional issues.

**Flagship Event System**
A conference marketing page with program tracks, day-by-day agenda, tiered registration, speaker showcases, sponsor visibility, and venue/travel logistics.

**CE / Resource Content Architecture**
A filterable, searchable resource library with pagination, category/type/CE filters, and structured detail pages for individual courses and publications.

**Chapter / Community Engagement**
A chapter finder with region and size filters, chapter detail pages with local events and leadership, and a community engagement model that connects local activity to statewide strategy.

**Career Center / Job Board**
A filterable job board with pagination, employer services, salary resources, and structured job detail pages with application pathways.

**Institutional Credibility**
A rich About page with mission/vision/promise, institutional scale stats, councils/committees, governance transparency, annual impact reporting, historical timeline, partner networks, and recognition modules.

**Support Routing / Contact System**
A contact page with multi-channel support paths, demo-safe form with validation, response process transparency, department directory, and quick-link navigation.

### Strategy Summary

A complete association website system designed to unify membership, advocacy, education, events, resources, chapters, and career support into a single professional platform — demonstrating how a modern association can present its mission, scale, and active work in a way that builds trust with members, sponsors, employers, students, and policymakers.

## 9. Technical Notes

**Framework:** Astro (static site generation)
**Styling:** Scoped CSS with CSS custom properties; `is:global` used selectively for hero components and dynamically injected elements
**JavaScript:** Vanilla JS with `is:inline` for filters, pagination, mobile menu, and FAQ accordions
**Data:** Centralized in `src/data/association.ts` with TypeScript types
**Fonts:** Inter (Google Fonts)
**Dependencies:** None beyond Astro core
**Build output:** 41 static HTML pages
